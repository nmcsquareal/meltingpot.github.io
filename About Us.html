About Us

The Melting Pot started its own place at the heart of Venice in Italy. We started as a small restaurant but we grow bigger and bigger as more and more people discover what they have never tasted before!


Today and into the future, we continue to serve thousands of hungry customers satisfying their cravings for delicious and filling meal.

Our Team

Chef Neal Centino (Head Chef)

Your private head chef Neal Dyxther Centino, CMB, has spent the last 12 years cooking and staging at some of the most recognized restaurants around the world including Michelin starred restaurants in USA, France, Italy, South Korea and Australia.

He was also a two-time recipient of the Chef's Choice Prize awarded in San Francisco City in California. He was also portrayed in different international magazine such as Forbes Magazine, Food & Wine Magazine, as well as Bon Apetit.

Chef Ero Lance Centino (Sous Chef)

Chef Ero Lance Centino has been a private chef for almost a decade. He is a former scholar and a graduate from The Culinary Institute of America, which is considered by some news and publications to be one of the best culinary schools in the world.

He is also an awesome youtube vlogger, in which he cooks and eats different variety of food all over the world. He has been perfecting every kind of dishes - from american, italian, french, and even asian cuisine!

Chef MJ Pagtalunan (Sous Chef)

Chef MJ Pagtalunan is a professional chef who specializes in baking as well as in desserts. His new version of a dessert, called Tiramisu Layer Cake, has been an article in a famous cookbook: Food Network Magazine.
